Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A classic comfort food favorite!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Steps:

  1. Butter the outside of each slice of bread.
  2. Place the cheese between the slices of bread.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides.
