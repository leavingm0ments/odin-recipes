Key Lime Pie

A refreshing tropical dessert!

Ingredients:

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter.
  3. Pack the mixture into a pie crust.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes.
  5. In a medium bowl, whisk together condensed milk, egg yolks, and lime juice.
  6. Gradually whisk in cornstarch until smooth.
  7. Pour the filling into the baked crust.
  8. Bake for 15 minutes or until set.
