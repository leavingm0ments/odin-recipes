Matcha Latte

A delicious Japanese green tea beverage!

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon matcha powder
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Steps:

  1. Whisk the matcha powder with hot water until smooth.
  2. Add milk and honey (if using) and whisk until well combined.
  3. Serve hot or cold.
